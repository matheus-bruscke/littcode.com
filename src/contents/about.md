I'm a software engineer passionate about user interfaces and developer experience. After more than five years surviving in this industry, I've come to define my work around three core principles:

- **Consistency:** Shipping a product that works gets you good reviews. Building a platform that behaves consistently gets you advocates.
- **Predicability:** Good software shouldn't fall apart because someone changed a button color. I'll happily die on that hill. Grow incrementally, grow predictably.
- **Clarity**: Whatever you leave in the shadows today will eventually grow large enough to block the light. Code, architecture, and documentation all benefit from being painfully clear.
